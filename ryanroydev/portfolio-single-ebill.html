EBILL Details

Project Date 2022
Role Web Application
Client SKY CABLE

The project aims to develop a comprehensive website that enables SkyCable subscribers to enroll in electronic billing, facilitating a seamless transition to a paperless statement of account (SOA) system. This initiative will not only enhance user convenience but also promote environmental sustainability by reducing paper waste. Upon visiting the website, subscribers will be guided through a straightforward registration process, where they can securely input their SkyCable account details. Once registered, users can easily opt into electronic billing, receiving notifications and monthly statements via email or SMS.

SkyCable not only enhances customer experience but also demonstrates its commitment to sustainability. Ultimately, this project aims to simplify the billing process, ensuring subscribers enjoy the ultimate convenience while contributing to a greener future.
