SkyFiber Alternet Topup Plan Details

Project Date 2021
Role API, Web Application
Client SKY CABLE

The Skyfiber Alternet Top-Up project empowers subscribers with flexible internet speed options tailored to their needs. Users can conveniently enhance their internet experience by choosing from various speeds—40 Mbps, 80 Mbps, or 160 Mbps—based on their usage requirements. This feature is designed to accommodate different online activities, whether it's streaming, gaming, or working from home. Subscribers have the freedom to select their top-up duration as well, with options of 5, 15, or 30 days. This flexibility allows users to adapt their internet services according to their specific needs, ensuring they only pay for what they use. The seamless integration of this top-up feature into the platform enhances user experience, making it easy to manage subscriptions and speeds without any hassle

With a user-friendly interface, subscribers can quickly navigate through the options and make their selections in real-time, enhancing customer satisfaction. The Skyfiber Alternet Top-Up project not only provides enhanced connectivity but also empowers users with control over their internet service, fostering a more personalized and efficient online experience. This innovative approach aligns with the growing demand for adaptable digital solutions in today’s fast-paced world.
