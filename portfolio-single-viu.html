Viu Activation Page Details

Project Date 2019
Role API, Microsite
Client SKY CABLE

The project aims to enable MySky subscribers to easily subscribe to Viu’s subscription service through a seamless voucher system. This innovative approach allows users to choose between one-time or recurring subscription options, enhancing flexibility and convenience. By integrating with MySky, users can manage their subscriptions securely, ensuring they have full control over their payment and personal information. The subscription process is designed to be user-friendly. Subscribers can select their desired Viu plan, whether they prefer a one-time voucher for occasional viewing or a recurring subscription for uninterrupted access to premium content. The system will facilitate automatic renewals for recurring subscriptions, sending notifications to users about upcoming charges and ensuring transparency.

Additionally, the integration leverages MySky’s secure data management capabilities, allowing users to track their subscriptions, manage their vouchers, and make adjustments as needed. This project not only simplifies the subscription process but also promotes user engagement with Viu’s extensive library of shows and movies. By combining MySky’s decentralized identity management with Viu’s content offerings, we aim to create a robust platform that prioritizes user experience, security, and flexibility. Ultimately, this initiative seeks to enhance viewer satisfaction and foster a loyal customer base for Viu through tailored subscription solutions.
